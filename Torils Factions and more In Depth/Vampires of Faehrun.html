The Croft Family

 

Lady Ayalin Croft

 

It is uncertain how old the lady is as she is currently one of the oldest vampires still in Faehrun and one of the most powerful night walkers out there. The one clue a person could get from how timeless she is, hail from the fact that she grew up during the rise of Cormy more than 3'000 years ago. Still not as old as dragons but impressive none-the less. Her age though is not what makes her one of the stronger vampires out there as that is due to her keen mind and blood line. Her blood line is one of the purest and thus her only vampire weakness is the sun it self and her vampire abilities are that of Tier 5 which is the strongest a vampire can get.

Lady Ayalin might be a mythical being of ancient times and one of the strongest vampires in existence but streangth comes in many shapes. She is not a fighter and she hate violance. If a fight is forced on her she will do so but it's not an outcome she would prefer. Her streangth comes due to her intelligence as she uses sweet words for diplomacy and history to her benefit. She makes deals with those she can trust and make offerings where she deems it necessary to do so. For example if a group of adventurers would threaten her life, she would instead rather to help the adventurers and be their allies. If that does not work she will offer them something she could part with for her life as she wants anything else but a fight.

 

Ever since she grew up in Cormyr she has always been a pacifist, choosing a path of diplomacy over violance and she studied history, art, litterature, music and much more as much she could find and learn. She loves romance novels and poetry and it's through her studies that she found her husband. A doctor by the name of Davian Croft. Like she he had a thirst of knowledge and studied all he could, the difference is that he also studied the arcane. Something she always admired and fantasised about but it was a gift she never could master. They studied together, drank fine wines due to his increasing popularity and knowledge of medicine and eventually the two would fall in love. Something that would end before it would last for the rest of their lives as Davian was a noble in Cormy he falling in love with Ayalin who to his families eye was a mere servant their relationship was frowned upon. Davian though did not care and wanted to visit the Sword Coast and go travel asked Ayalin of she wanted to run away with her. Ayalin agreeded and with none of them being that good fighters the ambush set up by the Croft family ended their lives then and there. Luckily for the two of them a friend of Davian was nere. He gave them the crimson gift and with the two of them dead they ventured west towards a new land where they could live happily ever after.

 

After the betrayal Croft started to learn how to fight and Ayalin used her contacts and political skill to garner a safe haven in the far north in a forest. Together the two moved into the Cold Wood into a small village. The years passed by and once Croft was ready he left Ayalin in charge of things at home while he went to Cormy to avange their deaths. Ayalin agreed and one night a woman holding a young elven boy arrived at her footsteps. Both were dying and Ayalin knowing nothing about medicine decided to give them some of her blood as the two gained the crimson gift. This is how the croft family gre w into two more. Davian was surprised but not opposed to the idea of expanding their family as afterall. He have gained a wife and now a lover which Ayalin was fine with. Afterall a doctor gains a lot of stress and two women is better at relieving him of it than just one who recently have gotten a noble title in Neverwinter and now often busy with the political game. Davian took care of things in Cormyr and Thalia who became Davians pupil was left in charge of the castle they had built in order to house the growing family.

 

Now days Ayalin is trying to gain favour with a war lord by the name of Drazzor and at the same time trying to stay on the new queens good grace as she sees the queen as one of her closest friends but Queen Esseren can not give her what she wants and that is a home in Neverwinter due to her condition. They are friends and thus the secret is known to the queen which puts her in a difficuily spot. If Drazzor would become king than she and her husband was promised a place at the castle for their help in favouring other nobles to his cause. Something Ayalin hopes to accomplish as she knows quite well how discrimination and hate can feel and she like Drazzor hopes everybody could live happily together no matter what they look like.
