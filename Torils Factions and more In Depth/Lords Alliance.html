Identity

 

About

The Lords alliance is and was the goverment of the sword coast for the longest of times. It is a faction made and formed by various nobility in the Sword Coast that are spread through out the land. Once a year they have a meeting where they discuss current affairs and they always work in the betterment of the sword coast. Every decision they made is made through vote and has a democratic system in place so that no city or place will be favored over another. They are the founders of the Unified military of the sword coast and work together in times of war to fight foreign nations. In peace time they work as sponsors for adventuring parties that go and do quests for the various nobles when dangerous threats are around. For example a noble could come to the faction and tell them about a close by beholder taking his citizens. The faction then make a quests for it that then adventurers pickup and gets a reward from. The reward is either given by the nobility himself or the faction as payment for the service. The reason that the faction is willing to pay is because a danger to one part of the sword coast could later become a problem to the realm. However most nobility pay the reward themselves cause when you have gathering of nobilities in a faction who are all looking for self interest it becomes political game and every time there is a unified kingdom it rocks this boat.

Many of the members inside the faction is against having a monarchy as it disrupts their trade and the faction.

 

Contacts

The Lords Alliance being both goverment and a guild when doing big operations such as war relies on their allies to make large moves and thus they have contacts with other goverments and guilds outside of The Sword Coast. Their closest allies are those fellow guilds who fight for good being The Harpers, Order of the Gauntlet and Emerald Conclave to name a few. They also have close ties to the city of Silverymoon and their Spellguard as well as connections with the neutral Almn to the south. Elturel is their closest military contact as in times of war has an alliance with eachother but the alliance between the elves of High Forest and the Lords Alliance their connections are tense. The other races that are friendly with them are the dwarves in the north. After all Mithral Hall and the dwarven queen is the dwarves direct contact with The Sword Coast.

 

Operations

When it comes to how the guild operates it is all dependant on scale for the guild. For example if there is a local threat around Triboar for example a guild member can grab a poster on their job board and carry out a mission like a normal adventurer in any guild. Somebody has issues with bandit and the mayor or lord has sent out a notice to have it fixed. However if it's a political matter the jobs are not posted and instead directly deliever to the people best suited for the task.

 

For larger jobs such as taking out a speicific monster or getting a rare ingridient for a store it is a bit more complicated. Little is known about this but most adventurers in the sword coast is actually illegal as these missions required you to have a registered party with a guild that is associated with Force Grey, Lords Alliance, Order of the Gauntlet or any other guild in order to do the mission legaly.

The reason for this like so many things are, is because of taxes ofc. Any mission or quest needs to be registered in a guild to be taxed and rewards are also taxed and regulated by the goverment of the Swordcoast. The best tax deals are made with the Lords Alliance since they are the actual goverment but they don't provide special skills or gear that other guilds do.

 

Goals

The Lords alliance is as much as a gorment of the sword coast as it is a guild as they hire adventurers from across Fahrun to protect the land against any and all kind of dangers. They often work with other guilds and helps turn the wheel with processing official business regarding trade to get things done. Because of them being the goverment they can provice special permissions to guilds and groups so that they can go to a place to kill a group of bandits that are harassing trade routes or go to a dragon lair and slay a dragon that is about to burn a city.

 

Public Opinion

The common folk revere the Lords Alliance like it's the only guild in the world as they are most well known and has the most iconic members as several nobles are part of the guild. Although none of the highmembers actually do any of the quests themselves, they are the poster childs and faces of the faction. You will see knights in shiny armour and fair ladies in fancy nobly clothes dressed as advneturers show casing the pristine and wonderous life of adventurers that can be had as a member. That is why adventurers are very well liked in cities and towns but it's also the very reason they are critiqued. Obviously it is all a farce and the grim reality of adventurers is overshadowed. None of those performerers are actual adventurers themselves and is just a PR stunt. Few of the actual leaders has any combat experience themselves and thus the guild is often made fun of by other adventurers belonging to other guilds.

 

Leaders

Dagult Neverember, Lord Protector of Neverwinter

Since Dagult Neverember was deposed as the Open Lord of Waterdeep, he has thrown his full attention and effort into the rebuilding of the city from which he claims descent.

Whatever people’s opinions are of his claim to Neverwinter’s throne, he has proven a capable, inspiring leader over these last few years, and the population has embraced him as Lord Protector.He engineered the sealing of the Chasm and the restoration of the High Road, and is seeking other ways to repair and improve the city. Even if he can never prove his descent from Lord Nasher Alagondar, the people of Neverwinter have accepted his leadership.

Dagult is a proud supporter of the current king of the Sword Coast despite his somewhat mixed noble court over the matter. The one noble that have spoken most ill of the king is a woman named by Eliza Amitel. She and some of her followed consider a king to be not necessary and to be a mere distraction. After all the sword coast have not had a king for hundreds of year and been just fine without it. However a minority they and not a democracy she has little to no power to make an actual attempt.
