Anduin Decker Brigadier

Anduin Decker was born into the Decker family the year 1476 DR and is currently in the 1506 DR at his prime at the age of 30 years old. The decker family is a big noble family hailing from the city of Mirabar and have a long line of soldiers of many ranks. They have a rich family with a big mansion carved into the northern mountain built more like a fortress than a normal hom.e It sits right next to the castle of Mirabar reminding everybody their strong position of protectors of Mirabar.

 

Anduin as the oldest son in the family has the highest rank in the sword coast military as Brigadier and is in charge of the many generals that can be found in the military.

During his upbringing he was a quiet person who minded his own business. In a big family with two other brothers and a sister as the oldest child he had a habit of taking a lot of burdens of himself. Anduin gifted in the art of combat and with his strong leading attitude does not often speak but when he does people tends to pay attention.
