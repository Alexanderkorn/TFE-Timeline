Queen Orvuisita Esseren

Queen Orvuisita Esseren was born 174 DR in the city of Marabar by the second ruling lord of Marabar. During her childhood she was born during a time of war as the invasion of the drow was going on during her upbringing. More of a tomboy and wanting to prove her self to her father learned how to fight and weild a blade. At a young age of 16 she ran away from home and joined the war as a squire.

 

During a decisive fight against the drow at Tyar-Besil she awakened her divine powers becoming a full fledged war cleric. With her newfound power and turning victiourius she quickly raised in her ranks. At the age of 24 fighting in the underdark she met her father once more and was brought back to the surface to be crowned queen as her father was at the end of his life.

 

She gladly accepted becoming queen and would after some time return to the war but at the depth of their progress sadly she lost her life at the age of 33 the year 207 DR.

 

1495 DR She was reborn by her patreon Lathander at the dawn of a time where she would be needed once more. She returned to her kingdom finding out that the last bloodline of her family has ended and she was the last remaining Esseren left in the sword coast. She took upon the crown once more and find out about the upcomming threat of dragons and their cultists by the vision Lathander granted her upon her revival. Lathander during the vision told her the following.

"I will bring you back into world of turmoil where fate hangs at the balance. You the last heir of Esseren shall fight the dragons of Tiamat and be a blockade to their troups. You shall wed a soldier who will save the world and then become king. When the kindom is once more safe you will once more pass at the end of a great war where your husband and children will carry on the line of Esseren."

As the prohecy said she thought agaonst the cult of dragons and eventually met Baldrich who saved the world against the dragon godess Tiamat and saved the world.

 

The two have now spent the past 10 years to unite the sword coast under a kingdom once more and with the war going Esseren fears this is tha last leg of her journey.
