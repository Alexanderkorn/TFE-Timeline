Brigadier Jackson Cole

Brigadier Jackson Cole is at the age of 47 and was born the year 1459 DR. The Cole family did not start as a noble house but later on became one through the great deeds they have made since their instatement into the military. This started with Jackson's father being enlisted by Garedd of House Esseren in Mirabar as a personal body guard and protector of the city. Jackson's father Mathias Cole have taken down bandits, an invading hobgobiln army orchestrated from Phandelver by the late now dead hobgoblin king. Due to the familys fame even though they were recruited from a poor none noble familiy when Jackson was young, his dad Mathias got promoted to Brigadier in the military of the Sword Coast. Now days Mathias is retired and his promising son Jackson is in charge.

 

Jackson during his upbringing is his fathers son. He always admired his father and always tried to live up to his fathers standards and unlike the troupe Mathias have supported him through his life. Due to this though his mother was left behind and with her husband always busy with work and her son chasing in his foot step. She and Mathias seperated. She now lives a comfortable life as a court woman by the name of Elizabeth in Mirabar.
