Ehrahni

Ehrahni is one of the seven ward from the feywild and one amongst seven sisters.

 

At a young age she quckly became the leader of her siblings and took on a somewhat parenting role for her other sisters. As the more responsible one she often made sure the other behavend and did as the king tree has taught them. She often was the voice of advise if anybody of them were in trouble or had concerns. However as a leader with no parents this was a double edged sword.

While her sisters could rely on her she herself had nobody for her self to rely on and took on the burden from her family alone. A burden she carries on her shoulds till this day and as they met the different fey courts she at times made deals on her sisters behalf and had to do something she is not proud of her in her adult life. When the split happened she chose the world of Toril and secluded and seperate she is in Toril still and do not plan on seeing her kin maybe never.
