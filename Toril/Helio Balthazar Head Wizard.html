Helio Balthazar Head Wizard

 

Helio Balthar is a Abjuration Wizard who graduated from Candle Keep quite a while ago. He was born 1454 DR and is currently working as the head advisor for the king and was for a brief moment a member of the arcane brother hood but only after a few years of servie he left and later got into the position he is in right now. He had the name of Helio the Purple. Not much is known about him and he was more of a loner during his class years as he likes to keep to himself and his studies when he is not working.
