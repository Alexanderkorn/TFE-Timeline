Astaroth The Kingly Advisor

 

Astaroth is a old gnome who is 362 years old. He was born in the year 1144 DR and was but a child back then. He grew up dreaming of being a member among the eigth learning about all their adventures. Once he got a lecture from Mordenkainend during his time at Candlekeep and it's a day he will never forget. However as he grew older he quickly learned the dangers of adventures and grew out of his child hood dreams. For the longest time he owned a small magic store. It became more of a part time job while he started studying alchemy and politics. As Astraroth become more involved in the politics of the sword coast eventually ended up being the kings advisor.

 

The Sword Coast have often from being a monarch to independant city states many times which facinated Astaroth and when he got the chance of being invited to join as an advisor for the new king he basically jumped at the oppurtunity. Dealing with Baldritch though he slightly regrets his decision.
