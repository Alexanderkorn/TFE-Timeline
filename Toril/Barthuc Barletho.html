Barthuc Barletho

Barthuc was born in the year 294 DR on the streets of Baldurs Gate. At a young age he was raised in an orphanage but unique as he is. He has always been a bit odd and a bit different. Not like the other kids who dreamed of being heroes or knights of a castle. When Barthuc grew up he kept to himself and read every single book he could find and was quite gifted in the arcane, quickly he learned to do things most teenagers could not do when he was still a kid. He never made any friends and was viewed as an oddball by the other kids as they often found him talking to himself or something else. This is because of a rare innate ability he was born with. Barthuc himself knew that the voices he heard were not fake or imaginary but were actual devils from the hells. Not knowing his parents and seemed to be born out of thin air, Barthuc reasoned that it might be related to his birth but never questioned it or looked into it like any normal person would do when they grew older.
