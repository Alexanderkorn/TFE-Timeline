Thorym The Mad

Thorym was born the year 1123 and is one of four brothers. Orryn the Chaos is the most known amongst his family Thorym went a different dirrection. Gifted in the arcane both went to Candlekeep and both excelled in their studies but one thing that seperated them, even from a young age is that Thorym was born with a defect. He was born mad and showed erratic behaviours seeing things that was not there and talking to creatures nobody else could see or hear.

 

Some people who are interwoven in the weave becomes sorcerers and those who are entangled in it becomes wild mages. Thorym was a different case entirely and during his time in Candlekeep he was the case study for many spell casters. Sick and mad but gifted in seeing the past, present, future and mind trapped in the ethereal. The studies showed that it was not like he could see the future but experiences it in first person. Like he was there during the terrible times but also the magnificant times of wonders.

He has a translocated mind so it was not that he is mad per say but when you are born questioning everything you see and not even sure when or where you are then it sure can drive you mad.

 

Despite his flaw Thorym excelled in his studies at Candlekeep and while communication was hard there was ways and time you could communicate. It was not the school that reached out to him but he himself studying using the resources around him as the school did nothing to teach him magic and only allowed the gnome to teach him self the magic needed to craft spells and to make an enchanted book that allowed two people to write to eachother across planes and regardless of distance. Thoryms Book of Communication. For the first time in his life Thorym could communicate with other through text regardless of where he found his mind to be.

 

During his childhood not much was understood about the child and the young Thorym was just acting mad. After showing his skill in magic he was sent to Candlekeep as he was studied. There he learned magic and make magic items but on the side Thorym was also given the assets needed to record and to study the magnificant creatures he took interest in. Giants! Since the chaos war to the dragon giant war and the giant civil war they have all been a big and important part in the history of Toril. Thorym learned this quite quickly living through the future and past and took it upon himself to study the creatures and their history in detail. Not many people know this as it's kept secret but the runic magic that exist is because it's magic that Thorym documented from the giants and shared with friends and fellow spellcasters cause as his condition was so rare and so unique many famous wizards and arcanists took interest in the gnome.

 

Born during the adventorous time of the 9 he was later visited by Mordenkainen himself once and several other spell casters that took interest in him. Another was named Barthuc Barletho who took an interest in the gnome. The two of them came to know each other quite well during this time and Thorym became an unofficial member of the Pentharam but Barthuc always kepts Thorym at an arms lenght as did the gnome towards him as the two never came to trust eachother fully. Little did Barthuc know that a certain Ezekel became closer with the gnome and instead of keeping a distance took a keen liking to Thorym and later on even got the gnomes help to make a lesser copy of himself that could stay around while Ezekel was elsewhere. The two worked together in secret for the longest time and eventually when the time was ready Ezekel copy left to form a mage guild in Baldurs Gate while Thorym after long ago leaving Candlekeep made his own business in Waterdeep as an expert on Giants for researchers and to this very day continues to record and study history where he sees it with his mind.

 

Much to many's demise the gnome has never recorded the future despite able to see it, stating that no good will ever come from knowing ones future. Knowing to much about history, Waterdeep considers Thorym a security risk and pays him well and in turn keeps his location city which Thorym partially agrees to as according to the gnome, only those smart enough and clever enough should be given the information he has as only those would be willing to use his knowledge for sensible things.
