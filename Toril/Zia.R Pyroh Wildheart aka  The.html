Zia.R Pyroh Wildheart aka The Butcher

Zia was born in the wilderness between Elturgard, Cormyr and Amn within her tribe. Her father was a woman goliath while his father was a human. It was a tribe of mixed races that lived of the land like in the oldern time from hunting and gathering. It was a small tribe that always stayed between 20 and 30 people. They were peacful and often traided in Elturgard at times and had a happy co-existence. At the fall of Elturgard their trade was shortlived and the tribe had to find new avenues. At first the tripe traveled south to Amn to maybe do some trade ain Eshputa but as they arrived they were greeted with hostility as they were treated as raiders and marauders. The clan instead left towards the north and was planning to traverse the mountains. As they arrived they quickly found them selved pinned between mountains and Amn as adventurers of Amn was hired to kill them off. Battle broke out and all but a few survived. Zia's father was one of the casualities. Some of them made it off and slank into the mountains. They knew how to fight and Zia was no difference. She however had other plans as she was filled with something she never had experienced before. A pure hatred and anger like that of fire as she grabbed her fathers axes and broke off from her tribe. The adventurers thinking this was a simple fight of taking out bandits did not expect them selves to be ambushed in the dead of night as Zia came on them like a whirlwind of anger and hatred as she killed the people resting. However as she looked around she noticed that only half of the 8 party members were at the camp. Panicked she tried to backtrack to find her tribe but it was to late. Once more her fury struck at the adventurers that had killed her tribe and her parents. When all was said and done at the edge of her own life she had survived but she was not yet done as she knew who was responisble for them being hired and her vengence was not yet sated.

 

The Butcher

After her tribe died Zia went to her first city as she was never in charge of trade as she did not know how to read or write. However she did not come on friendly terms as she made it towards the barracks before she started fighting as she got her vengence before eventually she would be apprehended. She was then taken to Athkatla as she was put into prison. She would not stay for long though as she would stay there until she found a point that she broke free and just like the nickname she was given she broke free with fellow prisoners and broke free. After that she would be known "The Butcher of Amn" Together with the fellow prisoners she and her group become the thing that Amn fears as they beceme marauders pillaging and raiding the various cities, villages and town. With Zia as their right hand woman they were so prosperous that they expanded from Amn to the Eltugard in the north. Cormy to the north west all the way east to Hlondeth and Sspech and along the Lake of Steam.

 

Turn of Events

After becomming a woman in her late 20's while raiding the now enterprise of marauders one day encountered a group of adventurers. None of them looks quite strong and as they attacked the group they would quickly learned that they were heavily outmatched. Zia though would not give up and took down two of them before she would be bested by a young looking wizard that bested her. She would not give up and to her surprise the group did not punish the marauders or took them to prison. Instead they taught them a lesson on who not mess with. Zia confused about this saw something she had not seen in a long long time. They reminded her of her old tribe and as they left she decided to take a longer look. She offered the tribe to spy on them and maybe find a new oppurtunity while in actuallity she would follow them and observe them. Till one day she was spotted by the young wizard and talked to him and after a long long discussion they ended things as friends.
