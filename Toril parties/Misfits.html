Rimi Wildclaw-Matreon of the Forest

 

Rimi grew up in the Border Forest as an orphan together with her best friends Ihra, Ayda and Tom who all stuck around and often got into trouble. Each of them had seperated druidic abilities as Rimi is a druid of the Shephard, Ihra is a moon druid, Ayda is a druid of the stars and Tom is a spore druid. They trained under their elder Lahrra who is a high elf that everybody calls Gran Gran despite her young looks. Easch of them had responsibilites as Ayda studied nature and history and was one day to be the next elder of the circle. Tom studied agrculture and food production and how to tend to crops while Ihra helped around with about everything. Rimi was in charge of taking care of all their animals and was their acolyte of the Wild Mother, Mellora.

 

When growing up Rimi had a pleasant upbringing and despite the many misschief often came up by Tom she did not pay to attention to their teachings and rather fooled around. She always wondered about her parents though and thought to herself that maybe the reason they left her there was because they did not love her. That's maybe why the abandoned her to the druid, something she never told anybody, not even to her closest friends. What she did tell them though is that she had a crush on Ihra as girls growing up often do. The problem was that her absoulute BFF also had a crush on him and the two had a big fight one day as Ihra choice Ayda over her. However as times passed on their young fling did not last as they then became friends once more.

 

One day people were panicking and there was a new smell in the grove as Rimi smelled fire. She tried to find her friends but instead found Gran Gran who told her that they already ran and she needs to flee as well. Rimi never having left the grove did as she was told and ran and ran and ran for a long time until she could run no more.
