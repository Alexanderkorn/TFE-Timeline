Tristan Foxfur

The School of Lumiflection represents a sophisticated branch of arcane study, dedicated to mastering the manipulation of light in all its myriad forms. From the subtle glow of a candle to the brilliant radiance of the sun, Lumiflectors are adept at bending, reflecting, refracting, and shaping light for a variety of purposes.

 

Philosophy

Lumiflectors view light as more than just a physical phenomenon; to them, it embodies knowledge, purity, and power. The mastery of light, in their eyes, is akin to touching the fundamental essence of truth and perception. They also embrace the concept of shadow, seeing it as an integral counterpart to light, rather than an opposing force.

 

Key Concepts and Abilities

Prismal Constructs:

The ability to solidify light into semi-tangible forms for creating barriers, tools, or weapons.

Radiant Manipulation:

Mastery over the intensity of light sources, enabling Lumiflectors to dazzle or blind foes, or shroud an area in darkness.

Refraction Mastery:

Skilled in bending light to create illusions, render objects invisible, or distort appearances.

Spectrum Vision:

A developed talent to see beyond the normal spectrum, perceiving ultraviolet and infrared light.

Shadowmancy:

The art of controlling shadows, shaping them into various forms for espionage, defence, or attack.

Solar/Lunar Channeling:

Harnessing the energies of the sun and moon to empower spells, heal, or unleash energy beams.

 

Teaching and Learning

Lumiflection is taught in unique academies, often situated in locations with extraordinary lighting conditions. These academies are centres of learning where students engage in rituals involving mirrors, prisms, and crystals, which are also essential tools for their magical practices.
