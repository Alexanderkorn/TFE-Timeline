Eliahna

 

Eliahna is one of the seven ward from the feywild and one amongst seven sisters.

 

At a young age Eliahna was always there for her sisters and protected them from the dangers of the feywild. Chasing away pixies, Fighting Nilbogs who made fun of them and made sure every single one of her sisters came home safe. She never saw her self as the leader but despite this could not help herself of being jealous of her sister Ehrahni who everybody went for with their problems and concerns. She was always there for her sisters so why did they all turn to her and she hated her self for theese feelings as she knows It's not like she made them choose her as their leader. At times she went to the king tree to talk about this problem but the tree that brought them to life never handed them the answer and instead gave them clues and hints on how to find the answer themselves. To this the king tree said to her. "Jealousy is nothing to be ashemd about. It is part of life and often hides once true feeling. Embrace it and confront it."
Wise words but no comfort as her heart still was left uneasy. Despite that she took the trees advice and confronted Ehrahni about it. However things go seldom your way when emotions runs wild and one thing lead to another as missunderstandings happened and the two sisters ended up fighting one another. Maybe she thought that she could prove that she was strong to be a leader. Maybe she wanted to prove that Ehrahni is not as strong as she is and maybe she just wanted to vent her frustration. The Paladin and her sister who was a fighter exchanged blows and with her powers with her fighitng saw victory but then Ehrahni did something unexpected. Her sister dropped her blade and took a fatal blow to her stomach as she then hugged her sisters who whispered something she will never forget.

"If being a leader is so important for you then who I am to question that. You are my sister and I rather let you kill me than me having to hurt my beloved family."

 

With that said she drops her weapon regretting her action and since then she never questioned her ever again. Because of the incident they grew closer. They eventually grew up and while facing the councils she never left her side during some tought times and hopefully she was able to share her burden of leadership.

 

When the split happened Eliahna went to Eberron as Ehrahni wanted to alone go to Toril. The reason she wanted to head there alone was so maybe she could become a leader as great as her sister. However instead she found herself in different circumstances. When she was in the Elden Reaches she met a traveling group of planar wanderer including a person named Barthuc. They became quite good friends and each of them shared stories with eachother and decided to become travel partners.

Together they traveled across Khyber and she was even offered to be taken to other planes. However she and her sisters made a promiss and she was going to stay there until she was satisfied. Instead she offered them to be taken to the feywild which was accepted as the group went back to the fey and met her sisters there who were done with their travels and adventurers. At the same time most of her siblings was home visiting and that is when she found out that Ember and Eshahna had become a couple together. This hurt her a bit as she have had feelings for Ember earlier but never acknowledged them earler. She was taught that you should not be with your sister and took it litteraly and only now realised that was only if you were relatives by blood. Because of this some drama insued as she had found out that she herself was target of admiration and love by their traveling cleric.

Flustered and blushing she turned down his advances and started feeling that feeling from such a time along ago and tried to leave without notice but Barthuc as intelligent as he was saw through her and tried to fix it. Things started to get even worse as opinions and experience differed. Eventually the party decided to leave before stuff got out of hand.

 

Eliahna went back to Eberron and continues traveling but got what she wanted eventually as she became a leading member in the Elden Reaches despite being a palladin and not a druid or a ranger. She took this role seriously and burried her self in the work that laid before her. Eventually war approcahed the horizon and orcs from the south approached the forest threatening the long lasting peace while elves and folk from the east also posed as a threat. Tension all across Khorvair rose until the 100 year long war finaly broke loose. She asked for her friends for help like that they have done to so many other worlds. What she did not know and expect that the party would be asked to help other members in Eberron and she like many others were fighting on opposite sides. Luckily she never had to face some of her close friends but she had heard that one of the members she made friends with had been felled. She was not there when the situation came to an end and instead took a distance and stayed away from all of that.
