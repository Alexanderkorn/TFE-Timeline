Alberich Dhurran of House Jade

 

Despite common belief and it's easy to believe that the dwarf has been in Eberron his entire life but Alberich is originally from the world of Terra.

In the northern mountains of Scandia and Germania in the dwarven kingdoms Alberich was born into the Dhurran family. Alberich had a fairly common upbringing in his youth. He had friend and a good clan family that took care of him. Rather than mining like some dwarves he took a liking to exploring and at a young age started to train in fighting. Instead of mining he would secure the already existing mines and clear them from any monsters he would find.

 

However one day he would find a strange mine not like the others. From the start he could tell that it was different as unlike normal mines the tunnel was smooth and showed no markings that tools have been used against the cave wall. What evern stranger was that it seemed like it was going on forever. Alberich expecting it to lead to a dungeon or a lair gathered his courage and went onwards. However what he found was not anything dangerous as he actually found a gateway into Eberron and as he was looking back he saw a dead end.

 

Alberich who just came out of the tunnel found no entrnace back to his old plane and had no choise but to continue. Alberich continued on his way and eventually made it out of the tunnel system to find Mror Hold in Eberron. However the dwarf would not discover to find friend as he was treated with suspision and people who did not belief his story when he tried to explain himself. Alberich might not have found a lair or a dungeon but he had found some combat as he was determined to show his iron. "Show me your Iron" or "Show me your Steel" Is a dwarven phrace and it means to challange somebody to a duel.

So when Alberich said "Let me show you all my iron." It's a universal phrase to a challange. The dwarves of Mror Hold accepted the challange and battle would commense. The challange was to beat 10 of their best warriors and Alberich beat twice as many. He wanted to fight more but at that point he was ragged and exhausted. Not pleased Alberich later on challanged every house and every dwarf that said they were a fighter and all but one fight he won.

 

The fight he would loose was against the current king but despite defeat Alberich would learn a valuable lesson and come to find him self close to the king. The king taught him that battle is not always necessary and that staying ones blade is just as brave as swinging it. Alberich at the time disregarded his words but respected the king as defeat against another dwarf is nothing shameful in dwarven society and is actually a good way to find a friend. After all anybody willing to risk their life in a dual is a respectable warrior. One of the kings daughters took also a liking to Alberich and with the bet that Alberich and the king had made. Alberich would put away his blade and learn the craft which Alberich in his younger days would refuse and never agree to. His daughter showed him the rope and was quite angry at Alberich for certain reasons.

 

Like said earlier, Alberich at a young age refused to sit idle and do blacksmithing and would never dream to mine. The kings daughter Bhurihne was the kings personal smith and as Alberich lost his fight and would start to learn blacksmithing she was tasked in instructing him. She quickly found out that Alberich was a natural at the proffession and was furious as all of this time Alberich could have been making things rather than destroying things. He had squandered his talents according to her and despite her furious anger at him, Alberich could do nothing but find the whole situation amusing and her anger only stoked the flames in his heart as he always fancied such passionate women. Despite her best effort to distance herself from him he would continue his pursuit in his dwarven stubborned until the two would grow quite close. However as their relationship grew warmer Alberich would find a new companion in the form of Octavius.

 

Mror Holds always welcome strangers into their fold as guests for trade and their coin is always apreciated but as soon somebody talks about moving in and is to stay for longer than a month the city turn it's somewhat darker side. It's extemely hard to get a house as new houses has to be carved or restructured if you want to live in central Mror Hold. If you are to move into a neibhour hood you have to earn the respect ofyou neibhours or they will heckle you and try and force you to move out.So when this tiny gnome would show up and try and get a job and house in Mror Hold there was a lot of pushback. However this gnome miight be tiny and look frauk I can assure you that he might be tiny but he is mighty as he would cause quite a commotion as the gnome summoned elementals that kept showing up and would almost cause a small war against this small gnome. At first Alberich and the king would hate the gnome as he was considered a coward not fighting his own battles. The kings daughter however saw it differently and realised how the gnome must have traveled the world alone and saw his intelligence. The gnome instead got arrested and later employed to be the queens advisor. After some time the gnome came to be close to the royal house and Alberich as well.

The two grew to become the bestest of friends eventually.

Also when Octavius asked to go on an advnture across the realms Alberich naturally accepted as well so he could protect his friends. The two joined The Pentharaam and went on a long journet traveling with Ezekel, Ardun, Tirona, Tristan, Miriam, Melisandra, Norabar and many more. After they had enough they then later returned as the adventure would arrive at the doorstep to Mror Hold it self. Tensions between the nations had grown even more severe. Cyre's power expanded and threatened the other nations until war would break out. A war that would last 100 years. Octavius who had family in Zilargo, Friends in Cyre and home in Mror Hold was quite conflicted about the turn of events. However the king who wanted nothing to do with this war told that for those who wanted might go to their place to fight while Mror hold would be sealed and safe. Octavius did not see his family that close to him anymore sided with Cyre. Alberich said his fareweell to Octavius and wanted to follow and help but stubborn as dwarves are stayed behind in the city.

 

Once the war was over his bestest of friend was distraught and Octavius told him about the horrors of the war but also of the sacrifice that Ezekel has made making him self public enemy nr 1. A common foe to keep their friends from going at eachother throat and also ending the war once and for all bringing peace to the world. With everybody in The Pantharaam going their seperate ways a long time of peace spread before Barthuc once more called for the gnomes help in something called project Arcadia.
