The Seven Wards

In the twilight between day and night during the everfledging shifting of coloured leaved in a grand oak at the edges of fey wild seven sisters were born with no parent nor ward inside the tree that brought them. Seven leaves of autum sprung alive not as driads but Eladrin elves born from fey magic and some might even say one of the great flowering that elves possesed. None of the sisters were related by blood but they all came from the same tree and thus treated each other as sisters and cared deeply for one another. They grew up under it's crown and called it the King Tree and maybe this was true or maybe they just liked the idea when they were young to be called princesses. The tree spoke to each and everyone of them like a father would to a child and tought them how to fight and survive. In turn the seven wards as the tree called them would protect the tree and care for it until it was time for them to leave theirs nests as fledlings and would explore the world beyond.

 

As time changed so does the seasons and as winter came to their part of the feywild the king tree fell into a slumber and wilt away. The seven wards mourned the king tree and as their tears hit the soil beneath their naked feet it encased the tree in ice as it then start to glow and bloom like it was midsummer bestowing the seven wards with gifts and abilites that no normal elves have. They can talk with trees and beasts at will and they do not age. Each and every one of them was also given special gifts matching their personalities. Together the seven sisters traveled the feywild and together they adventured using what their father had taught them.

 

1-There was Ehranni the fighter. Strong and confident, A leader and the one who looked over her sisters.

2-There was Edhani the druid who were loving and the one who cared for her sisters.
3-There was Ehrennrei the alchemist who explored the lands and made remedies and potions for her kin.

4-There was Eliahnna the Paladin who guarded the grove and protected the family.

5-There was Edrhiin the astout who learned all there was to know about all around her to prepare her family what was to come.

6-There was Eshahna who loved nature and later falls in love with her sister Ember.

Ember was passionate and loves the art and lifter all her sisters spirits in time of need and late became a couple with Eshahna.

 

Together they explored the feywild and learned about the amazing and horrible things the realms offers to adventurers. They thought against beasts, monsters elementals and more in their travels and the last thing they discover would change their lives forever. As they eventually have travelled the fey wonder they arrived as civilisation with the courts. Something some of them took interest in but others took wary of. Together they explored the various courts but never staying longer than a week. On their journey they learned about other planes and that their fairly small world was getting bigger and bigger and even bigger. With such wide world and the different problems across the world they felt a responsibility to protect those who could not protect them selves and to scatter to gather information and then one day when they restless souls would be at peace and finish adventuring they will once more gather at the tree that brought them alive such a long time ago. They all swore an oath to eachother and afterwards all most of them scattered the multi-verse. After times some returned to the feywild to wait for their sisters to return and at times they came back but only for shorter times. The current year of 1506 there are 3 of them in the fey wild while the other continues to adventure to this day and who knows what the future holds.
