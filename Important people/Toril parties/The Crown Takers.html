The Crown Takers

Drazzors Rebelion: Tales of Crowns

 

A long time ago there was a kingdom along the Sword Coast named Uthtower. When the first king died his twin sons came into power and due to that fact they needed two crowns for the two kings. They went to the local arch wizard Inierv that would make them two identical crowns with powerful magic in their separate ways. Each of them having powerful protection magic and none divination magic such as a permanent spell of non-detection. However the crowns true potential would only be revealed when two twins would bear the crowns. The years passed by and eventually the second orc war commenced as orcs invaded from the north. As the kingdom was once more in danger the kings went to Inierv for help as to their surprise they discovered that the arch wizard has turned himself into a lich. However Inierv decided to help them and in doings so rose the tide and created a massive tidal wave. A tsunami that destroyed the entire orc army as well drowning the entire army of the kingdom and everybody living in it, creating the Mere of Deadmen.

 

After the kingdoms fall the crown was lost and there was to be no sign of what happened to the old kingdom or Inierv himself. Now two dragons by the name of Wezlum and Welzour seek the two crowns. For about a centuary or two the have made an alias by the name of Voaraghamanthar and has hired Alfarious tribe of lizardfolk and the cult of the dragon to find the second crown as they have already found one. Little do they know that Inierv placed a terrible curse on the crowns. If they were to find the crowns Inierv who is very much still alive could dominate the dragon under his will and control them both for whatever purpose he desires.

 

It has been 10 years since the defeat of Tiamat. With the recent death of the hobgoblin king a bugbear by the name of Drazzor has rose to claim the title of king of liberations as he seeks to conquer the Sword Coast and claim rights and citizenship for all monster races, including goblins, lizardfolk and bugbears. His rebelion is in full swing and with the displeasure of the king and a kingdom an open rebelion have emerged in the Sword Coast as only the gods knows how it all will end as alliaced shifts and battles are thought.
